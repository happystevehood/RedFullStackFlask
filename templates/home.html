Welcome to Redline Fitness Games Results Explorer

This site provides a searchable and interactive view of the results from the Redline Fitness Games, covering both the 2023 and 2024 events. Data has been collected directly from the official timing provider, and organized for ease of browsing, filtering, and visual exploration.

Use the Search page to quickly find individual competitors or events by name, or head to the Results section to filter by year, gender, category, or location.

Try our Redline competitor search or view filtered event results by year and category.

The original race result pages are available from Racetec Results, and the official Redline Fitness Games website can be found at redlinefitnessgames.com.
